Welcome to Threads

The style guide for those working in marketing and looking for style inspiration for the working week.

Simply choose your sector below to unlock your new work wardrobe!

The Creative

As a creative you have the license to be bold and colourful. Rayban glasses are optional.

The Designer

A leather jacket is compulsory for any designer, teamed with an outfit for drinks after a day in the studio.

The Suit

Account people don't wear suits, except for in Mad Men. An 80% black outfit is obligatory (colour is reserved for the handbag or coat only.

     

Client-Side Corporate

Power dressing isn't just a choice - it's a way of life for those that have joined the dark side, working in a bank.

Client-side FMCG/ Retail

You'd certainly feel out of place in a suit - opt for the kind of dresses that Kate Middleton would wear on her days off!

Client-side Fridays

Dress Down Friday has finally arrived and you can wear jeans and jumpers!
