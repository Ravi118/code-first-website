Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I buy the outfits shown on the site?

A: Threads is an independent fashion blog, but we do include links to the retailers for any products that we feature on the website. Simply click on the item of clothing to link to the retailer.

Q: How often do you update the website?

A: We update this blog every week so you're never short of some work style inspiration.

Q: How do I know whether I'm dressing too old or young for work?

A: If you work within an agency, then dress to look as young as possible, as most of your colleagues will be under the age of forty. If you work client-side, then you should aim for older so your colleagues will take you more seriously ;)

Q: I work at an agency, but what do I wear to meetings with my clients?

A: That's easy - just mirror their style. If you're an agency person, dress more formal to attend client meetings. And if you're a client, wear skinny jeans and a leather jacket when spending the day at the agency!
